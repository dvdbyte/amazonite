About Us

Statistics show that females hold only 22% of positions in STEM fields within Nigeria. This gender gap translates into a lack of digital skills and confidence among many female entrepreneurs. This often leads to a reliance on male counterparts for these vital tasks, hindering their independence and growth. This is where Digital Amazonite comes in.

Digital Amazoniteis a passionate organization dedicated to bridging the digital skills gap for female undergraduate entrepreneurs across Nigeria. We believe in the power of technology to empower females and unlock their entrepreneurial potential.
